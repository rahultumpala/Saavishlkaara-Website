About Us

Talent is a gift. Skills are acquired, and when put together with hard work, the yield of success is assured.

We are a group of students who understood that learning only in class isn’t going to make any student better. Learning few tricks out of the class makes them the best. We learnt this truth and we want to make sure everyone knows the right path to success and so here we are. We also believe that when people catch the right path at a young age, they will understand this world in a better way and that is why our teaching starts from the age 12.

Saavishkaara has diagnosed the void between theory being taught and its application in the industry. It acts as a bridge between these both by imparting the required skills particular to the field(s) of students, and providing exposure to the real time projects and industry applications.

Trainers ensure that quality knowledge is imparted to the students, which is applicable to the real world scenarios.

Saavishkaara also helps students to find like-minded peers and help them complete their projects in a more comprehensive manner.

We conduct various workshops, training sessions, internships and provide skilled people for required tasks.
